About me


Hi! I'm Mary Joanne Pauline C. Arnante - responsive web developer, licensed interior designer and graphic designer. I am an avid learner of a variety of fields such as the arts, sciences, and philosophy. All the theories and concepts that I've learned from such a diverse array of fields keep my work inspired and exemplary. She always strives to be insightful and creative with her work, with culture and innovation playing influential roles in the way she creates. Apart from design and the arts, she is also passionate about innovations in technology, education and social service. She would like to join (or maybe start) a startup someday with her friends.

My design philosophy which inspires all my works is succinct but comprehensive: Design with thought. Effective design is data processed and realized in its most fluent form.
